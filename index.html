What is Drift?

A focused practice space to improve everyday conversations—without the pressure of being there live.

  • Simulated scenarios: conference intros, coffee chats, cold outreach
  • Real‑time tone & emotion feedback (Hume)
  • Actionable suggestions and quick retries

Simulated scenarios

Practice the moments that matter—networking, interviews, sales intros—safely and on your schedule.

Real‑time feedback

Instant signals on tone and delivery so you can adjust warmth, energy, and pacing in seconds.

Built‑in coaching

Targeted tips for phrasing and follow‑ups, with progress you can track over time.

How it works

  1. Pick a scenario you want to practice
  2. Speak naturally—Drift analyzes vocal cues in real time
  3. Get feedback, iterate quickly, and build confidence

Who it’s for

  • Students & grads prepping for fairs and interviews
  • Professionals building networking skills
  • Teams training for sales, support, or client comms

Privacy & data use

We use voice data only to provide feedback for you. You can delete sessions at any time. We do not sell your data. Full policy will be published at launch.
