Practice conversations. Get better, faster.

Drift is an AI‑guided practice space for small talk and networking. We simulate realistic social scenarios and provide real‑time tone & emotion feedback (powered by Hume) so you can improve confidence and delivery without the pressure of a live audience.

Request beta access See details

Who it's for

  • Students and grads prepping for career fairs & interviews
  • Professionals building networking confidence
  • Teams training for sales, support, or client comms

What you get

  • Live tone insights — warmth, energy, clarity
  • Scenario library — conferences, coffee chats, cold intros
  • Actionable tips — pacing, phrasing, follow‑ups
  • Progress tracking — see improvement over sessions

How it works

  1. Pick a scenario you want to practice
  2. Speak naturally — Drift analyzes your vocal cues
  3. Get immediate feedback and retry with targeted cues
